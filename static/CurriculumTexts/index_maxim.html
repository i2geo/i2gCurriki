Curriculum text collection of i2geo

Please choose a country to view its list of curriculum texts

Explanation. A curriculum text can be one of two things. It can be a document outlining a list of competencies and topics, such as a list of conditions a student has to fulfill in order to graduate from high school. Or it can be a program of study, for example a table of contents of a highschool textbook. Via this page, you can browse a collection such documents. Attached to them are links to searches on the i2geo platform, so you can quickly find resources that are relevant for your class.
